Introduction

We aim to work as a team and create new heights in terms of game development. The 3D animations and graphic designs add to our expertise in which we want to bring about new modifications and change the general notion of game development. We will uplift the area of work with new ideas about the development of game and try to make it one of a kind. We would like to offer a new gaming experience to our users so that they can enjoy and educate themselves about our culture and heritage at the same time.

Adobe Mixamo

Mixamo has been used in this game to create different characters and apply animations to all the characters in the game. The visual effects and skin alterations added to the feasibility of using this platform as a graphics designing element.

Adobe Fuse

Adobe Fuse has been used for the rigging of different characters of the game and applying costumes based upon the functions they perform in the game. The weapons have also been rigged wherever necessary.

About Us

We are a team hailing from Kolkata. Our prime focus in the world of game development is to offer something new to our users. In this project, our idea is to create a scene for gamers of this generation so that they can understand our glorious past and also enjoy the mythological action. The users will be able to enjoy the vast action sequences and also get to know the historical stories in a playful manner.
