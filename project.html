Snapshots of Game


Scene 3: Cave

Laxman is entering the cave in order to search for weapons so that he can kill Meghnad. Lord Sword is kept inside the cave.

Scene 3: Fight

Laxman is surrounded by the demon army and he gets ready to fight them with the help of the divine weapons and Vanar Sena.

Scene 3: Underwater

Laxman enters the underwater area where he has to survive from the attack of the demonic sharks. The longer he stays underwater, the greater his health will decrease.

Scene 3: Seashore

Laxman approaches the seashore to find more weapons and add them to his inventory. The fight ensues between the demon army and Vanar Sena.

Scene 2: Mountain

Laxman approaches a demon in the foot of the mountain. The demon in this scene has to be killed to proceed to the next.

Scene 2: Weapon

Laxman searches for his weapon to kill all the demons. After that he has to find Bibhisan.
